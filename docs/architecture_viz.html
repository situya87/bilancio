2. Kalecki Ring Scenario

Ring Topology

Parameters

SymbolNameControls
κ (kappa)Liquidity ratioCash / Total debt. Lower = more stress
cConcentrationDebt inequality (Dirichlet). Lower = more unequal
μ (mu)Maturity skew0 = early due dates, 1 = late
ρOutside mid ratioVBT price anchor. <1 enables buy trades
nRing sizeNumber of firms/households
MReserve multiplierBank reserves as fraction of deposits

Output Metrics

δ (delta)
Clearing rate — fraction of debt repaid
φ (phi)
Default rate — fraction of debt defaulted
total_loss%
Combined payable + deposit losses / S_total
trading_effect
δ_passive − δ_active (dealer impact)
CB_destr%
Central bank reserve destruction
n_defaults
Count of defaulted agents

Daily Phase Sequence

0
Setup — Create ring, mint cash, assign payables
One-time: burn bank cash, mint reserves (banking arms)
1
Dealer Trading — Secondary market for debt tickets
Sync state → eligible sellers/buyers → execute trades → sync back
2
NBFI Lending — Non-bank lender offers credit
Borrow-vs-sell decision, kappa-aware pricing
3
Bank Lending — Banks issue loans, create deposits
Treynor pricing, credit risk loading, capacity check
4
Settlement — Pay due obligations (MoP order)
Cash → Deposits → Reserves; rollover; default cascade
5
Interbank — Bank loan repayments, interbank clearing
Reserve transfers, CB loan repayments
6
CB Backstop — Top up bank reserves to target
Automatic CB lending, quote refresh
↻ repeat until stable or max days
W
Wind-Down — CB freeze, bank loan maturity
Banks repay CB from loan proceeds; default if can't
F
Final Settlement — CB collects all outstanding
Write off unpaid CB loans, compute CB reserve destruction

7 Comparison Arms

① Passive
Baseline — pure settlement, no intermediaries
Households only
② Active (Dealer)
Secondary market with dealer + VBTs
+ Dealer, VBTs
③ Lender (NBFI)
Non-bank credit facility
+ NonBankLender
④ Dealer + Lender
Both dealer and NBFI active
+ Dealer, VBTs, Lender
⑤ Bank + Passive
Banks only, no secondary market
+ Banks, CentralBank
⑥ Bank + Dealer
Banks + secondary market
+ Banks, CB, Dealer, VBTs
⑦ Bank + Dealer + NBFI
All intermediaries active
+ Banks, CB, Dealer, VBTs, Lender
